The Proposition
🔬

Research Gap

No business school globally hosts a dedicated electoral integrity research centre. ICBS governs a unique intersection: institutions, AI governance, and democratic market function.

⚙️

Live Infrastructure

ELIS v2.0 is not a proposal — it is running. 35 years of evidence (1990–2025), 8 database APIs, CI-enforced governance, PRISMA 2020 compliant. Ready to scale under a lab umbrella.

🌍

Global Relevance

70+ elections in 2024 alone. AI-generated disinformation, contested results, institutional erosion. Evidence-based strategy is the missing layer between academic research and policy action.
